History of Sagarmatha FC

What started as a group of boys having fun in a park playing football gave birth to SFC. The year was 1996 and a group of boys residing around Marrickville used to gather together to play football (original term) on the weekends because of their love towards the Beautiful Game. Despite our busy schedules with studies and work. the hoy of playing football helped us reinvigorated our dampened spirits.

Similar, there used to be another stronghold of Nepalese boys resing in the Starthfield-urwood area who had equal affectionate toward the game.
